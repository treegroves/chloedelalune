About

Hi there! My name is Chloe Or, also known by my artist handle Chloe de la Lune. I am an illustrator and portrait artist based in Auckland, New Zealand. I love to tell stories with my artwork, using subjects like people, animals and flowers with a cute or fantasy twist to intrigue and connect with the viewer. My art is created with a range of traditional and digital media such as oils, markers, Photoshop and the Procreate app.

I graduated from AUT majoring in pictorial studies in 2011, and has since created wordless children’s picture book for Imagistory called ‘My friends the penguins’, and various illustration for children’s event posters at Auckland Libraries. I was an artist exhibitor at Chromacon 2019, Zinefest 2017 – 19 and Overload 2018, and was the artist-in-residence at St Heliers Library on behalf of the Chromacon 2019 festival.

If you would like to work with me, I would love to hear from you!
